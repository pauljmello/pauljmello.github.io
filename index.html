About Me

Hi, I'm Paul! A PhD student at the University of Nevada, Reno researching the fundamentals of artificial intelligence. My passion is extracting, optimizing, and generalizing the fundamentals of AI systems through the lens of information. Currently, I am developing methods for improving model generalization in high-dimensional feature manifolds where observational samples are limited.

For collaborations or questions, feel free to reach out via my email or socials.
