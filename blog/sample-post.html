Why Fundamentals Matter

Before you can build advanced models, it's crucial to have a strong grasp of the basics. This includes understanding concepts like:

  • Linear algebra
  • Probability and statistics
  • Optimization techniques
  • Basic neural network structures

These topics provide the foundation for building more complex systems and allow for a deeper understanding of how AI models work.
